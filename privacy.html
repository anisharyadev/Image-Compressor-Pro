Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

Important Note: Image Compressor Pro processes your images entirely in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Because all image processing occurs locally in your web browser:

  • We do not upload, store, or have access to your original images
  • We do not store any copies of your compressed images
  • We do not collect any metadata from your images
  • We do not track which images you process

2. Data Processing

2.1 Image Processing

When you use Image Compressor Pro:

  • Your images are loaded directly into your browser's memory
  • All compression and optimization happens on your device
  • The processed images remain exclusively on your computer
  • No image data is transmitted over the internet during processing

2.2 Browser Storage

We may use minimal browser storage to:

  • Remember your preferred compression settings for convenience
  • Store anonymous usage preferences (like dark mode selection)

This data remains on your device and is not collected by us.

3. Analytics

To improve our service, we may collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • Which features are used most frequently
  • Approximate file sizes processed (without accessing image content)
  • Browser type and version for compatibility purposes
  • Aggregate statistics about compression ratios

This data cannot be used to identify individual users or their images.

4. Cookies

Image Compressor Pro uses only essential cookies for:

  • Maintaining user session preferences
  • Remembering your settings between visits

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may include:

  • Web fonts from Google Fonts (anonymous usage data may be collected by Google)
  • Hosting services that may log standard connection data

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at me@aryadev.in
